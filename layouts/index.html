How can I help?

If you are a student at Utah Valley University and have a love of liberty, or just want to learn more, please join our club. We need a few more members to be able to re-charter our official status at UVU. You can join the club by going to the Clubs office at UVU, which is in the new Student Live building, and is right next to the ballroom in the Sorensen Center. The official club dues are $5 USD.

If you are not a student at UVU, we can still use your help. Any participation to organize events, spread materials, and engage the community is appreciated. You can contact us on our Facebook Group or subscribe to our mailing list.

We are in need of funds for activities. Donations can be made by check, please contact us to do so. Or we can accept Bitcoin with the address 12seF2v4mhyC5Sq9Lb8VWFPpz3cvpufmts.

Educational Links

Linked here are websites with local and general information about libertarianism.

  • Young Americans for Liberty – America's premier collegiate liberty organization.
  • Libertas Institute – Utah legislative and educational group.
  • Ludwig von Mises Institute – Austrian Economics educational organization.
  • Foundation for Economic Education – One of the oldest free market organizations in the United States.
  • LewRockwell.com – Libertarian commentary and articles website, blog, and podcast.
  • Liberty Classroom – History and Economics classes, what you didn't learn in school.
  • Ron Paul Institute – Foreign policy and civil liberties educational organization.
  • Tom Woods Radio – Commute-sized daily interviews and speeches about all worthy liberty-related topics.
  • AntiWar.com – Anti-War, pro-peace news.
  • The Tenth Amendment Center – States Rights and Nullification movement.
  • Liberty.me – Social publishing site for libertarians.
  • Bitcoin – The Future of Money.
  • Electronic Frontier Foundation – Digital and Internet rights education and legal counsel.
